Home Affairs

Home Affairs

The first episode of MUGAR Season Two sets off with a bang to set the tone for the entire series.

In this episode, Mugar is waiting in a long line at the Home Affairs Office. He loudly complains about the long wait time when a man interrupts him. This sets Mugar off on one of his signature rages, leading to the large scale massacre and subsequent police intervention in the matter.
Mugar must fight his way through the hordes of cops and escape to the only place he knows is safe. The Vaal. Unbeknownst to Mugar, his business is unfinished when the man he argued with turns out to be someone completely different...
